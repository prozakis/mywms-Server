LOS Processes

Workstation

LOS Processes is a module that provides basic processes needed in a warehouse. The main processes can be reached by the menu item Window and include:

  • Create Advice: To announce incoming goods
  • Goods Receiption: To receive incoming goods
  • Create Order: To place a (customer or internal) order of goods for goods out
  • Treat Order: Manually arrange which stock units should be taken to fulfill an order (if not done automatically)

Further processes are designed to help organizing your warehouse. They can be accessed by menu item Actions and include:

  • Transfer Unit Load: Transfer a Unit Load to a given destination
  • Transfer Stock Unit to Unit Load: Transfer a Stock Unit from one Unit Load to another
  • Change amount: Change the amount of a given Stock Unit to increase or decrease the number of articles within one unit

The above mentioned processes are handled within the LOS Client user interface. Additionally there is a mobile web application for handling those processes that are operated by workers in the warehouse, typically by means of a mobile terminal.

Mobile

This application can be started with the menu item "Window-Mobile Processes".

The mobile processes include:

  • Mobile Goods Receipt: Handle receiption of goods with a terminal
  • Storage: Store goods in the warehouse
  • Picking: Retrieve goods from the warehouse to fulfill an order
  • Shipment Shipment of goods provided in the goods out area
  • Replenish Create Replenishment orders for fixed assigned pickinglocations
  • Stocktaking Handling of counting orders
